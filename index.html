Macbook Pro

The MacBook Pro is a high-performance laptop computer designed by Apple. It is available in 14- and 16-inch models, both with Retina displays. The MacBook Pro is powered by the M1 Pro or M1 Max chip, which offer up to 10x faster CPU performance and up to 40x faster graphics performance than the previous generation. The MacBook Pro also has a long battery life, up to 21 hours on a single charge.

Pixel Speaker

The Pixel Speaker is a smart speaker developed by Google. It has a sleek and minimalist design, and it comes in four colors: white, black, charcoal, and coral. The Pixel Speaker has a powerful sound system with two woofers and one tweeter, and it supports spatial audio for a more immersive listening experience. The Pixel Speaker is also a smart home hub, so you can use it to control your smart lights, thermostats, and other devices. You can also use it to get information, play music, and more. The Pixel Speaker is powered by Google Assistant, so you can control it with your voice.

JBL Charge 3

The JBL Charge 3 is a portable Bluetooth speaker with powerful stereo sound and a built-in power bank. It is waterproof and has a durable design, making it ideal for outdoor use. The Charge 3 has a long battery life of up to 20 hours on a single charge, and it can also be used to charge your smartphone or tablet.
